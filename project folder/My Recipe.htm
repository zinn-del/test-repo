How to make chicken pizza

image

The average slice of pizza contains 12 grams of protein. Pizza can also help you absorb the antioxidant Lycopene. Fresh veggies are one of the healthiest toppings!!

Ingridents

  • Pizza dough
  • Grilled marinated chicken breast
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese(mozerella)
  • Chilli or Jalaperoes(optional)
  • Cornmeal(To help slide the pizza into pizza stone)(optional)

    • Preparation

    1. Chop your grilled marinated chicken breast to prepare the topping
    2. Chop your chilli pepper
    3. Shape the dough into a circle using the pizza dough and the cornmeal
    4. Add your tomato sauce,cheese andyour desired toppings
    5. Bake your pizza and enjoy!!
